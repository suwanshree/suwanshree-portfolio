IDOONA: App Design and Build

IDOONA is a sustainability SaaS that guides companies through ESG data entry, scoring, and reporting. Teams answer tailored questionnaires, upload KPIs, and watch the platform turn raw figures into clear dashboards and progress insights. A custom LLM and benchmark algorithm convert metrics into an actionable ESG score, highlighting gaps and suggesting decarbonization steps. Explore it here: app.idoona.eu.

Platform Build

I contributed across the stack: React front-end with accessible components, Node/Express APIs, and data flows against MongoDB. The product transforms uploads and survey results into benchmarked KPIs, enabling shareable reports and steady iteration toward compliance.

Logo Animation

This logo animation was created for motion branding and pitch material. Deliverables spanned research, storyboards, vector prep, timing, and export to H.264. The piece extends the product’s visual language into motion while staying lightweight for presentations.
